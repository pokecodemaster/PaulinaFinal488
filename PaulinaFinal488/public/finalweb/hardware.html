Hardware


The touch panel controls the A.V. system in the rooms. You can route the desktop computer, your own laptop and the document camera up to the projectors and have the image of the source you choose on the screens.


The rooms are equipped with high definition mounted ceiling projectors. You can also control them using the touch panel. You can either power them on, off or blank and unblank them.


There is a desktop computer installed along with the A.V. system. The touch panel also works as the monitor for the computer. Just press View Instructor PC. The receiver for keyboard and mouse is plugged into the computer ready for you to use.


Touch panel in computer view.


Microphones may not be needed in all rooms as they are not big enough for them, however, they are available in all of them should instructors need them for teaching when they are losing their voice. We want everyone to have a wonderful experience everyday at Smith School of Business.


Microphones and receivers are available in every classroom and conference room. Students, faculty and staff can count on receivers, wireless handheld and lavalier, and wireless handheld microphones. We let people choose whatever works for them, so we provide different microphones to use.


Document cameras are also available for those who like to write directly on a document.
