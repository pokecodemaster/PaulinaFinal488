Get Started


The first thing you need to do is start up the system. This means that you need to power on the projectors, drop down the screens and power on the lights in the room you are in. You can do all this with the aid of the touch panel.


How to start up the system:

  1. Press the touch panel to wake it up.
  2. Press either Room PC, VGA 1, HDMI 1 or Doc Cam.
  3. Press Send to: Left Projector button.
  4. Press Sendo to: Right Projector button.

The A.V. system will power on both projectors, drop down the screens and dim the lights. You can also control the lighting in the room by pressing Lights/Shades button. Press any button under Lights and choose the one that works for you.


How to shutdown the system when you no longer need it:

  1. Press Normal Shutdown button.
  2. Press Yes when asked "Are you sure you want to shutdown?".

The A.V. system will power off the projectors and roll up the screens. The lights can be powered off manually using touch panel or the switch on the wall.
