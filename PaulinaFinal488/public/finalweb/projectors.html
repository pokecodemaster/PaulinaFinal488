Projectors


All classrooms and conference rooms have LCD projectors. The connection needs to be wired. These are mounted ceiling projectors. VGA and HDMI cables cannot be connected directly to them.


The touch panel controls the A.V. system in the rooms. You can route the desktop computer, your own laptop and the document camera up to the projectors and have the image of the source you choose on the screens.


If you want to connect your laptop, PC or Mac, the table below shows what equipment is needed to do so.

Computer (PC or Mac) VGA
HDMI
miniHDMI
Thunderbolt adpater


How to display desktop computer image on screens:

  1. Press Room PC button.
  2. Press Send to: Left Projector button.
  3. Press Send to: Right Projector button.

How to display laptop image on screens using VGA cable:

  1. Press VGA 1 button.
  2. Press Send to: Left Projector button.
  3. Press Send to: Right Projector button.

One end of the VGA cable goes into the computer and the other end into the A.V. system. Sometimes the connection is automatic on the laptop. If not:

  1. Righ click on desktop on your laptop.
  2. Select Screen resolution.
  3. Choose a display on Multiple displays.

Duplicate mode means that the image on the laptop is going to be the exact same on the screen. Extended mode means that you can see one image on the laptop and a different one on the screen. This works when working on two different things at the same time.


VGA cable connected to laptop


How to display laptop image on screens using HDMI cable:

  1. Press HDMI 1 button.
  2. Press Send to: Left Projectr button.
  3. Press Send to: Right Projector button.

HDMI cable connected to laptop

The connection works the same with a Mac computer. The only thing that varies is the use of a thunderbolt for VGA or miniHDMI for HDMI. Mac computers do not have VGA or HDMI inputs. A Mac adapter is connected directly to the computer and the VGA or HDMI cable directly to the adapter.


If the connection does not happen automatically:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Select Displays.
  3. Select Mirro.

If you do not want people to see what your personal information while you get ready for a class, presentation or meeting, you can press Blank/Unblank to blank the projectors. When the buttons are greeen, this means that the projectors are on, but the image is blocked and it is not displayed on screens. Once you finish setting up, you can press Blank/Unblank buttons again to unblank the projectors. Now people will be able to see the image on screens.


Some VGA cables come with an audio cable attached. If audio is going to be needed, the audio cable goes directly to the audio port in the laptop computer. If the VGA does not have one attached, an external audio cable would be needed, otherwise, if a video is going to be played, it would not be heard.


HDMI cables do not need an audio cable. Once the HDMI cable is connected, both video and audio will work and with a much better quality than using a VGA cable.
