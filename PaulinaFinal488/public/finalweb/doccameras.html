Document Camera


Document Cameras are placed on the desk where the touch panel is. Many faculty members still like to write on documents as they teach. Document cameras are perfect for this method of teaching, and they are very easy to use.


They can also be used to show photos, maps, handouts, worksheets and any other document any student, faculty and staff would like to show with others.


How to use the document camera:

  1. Press Doc Cam button.
  2. Press Send to: Left Projector button.
  3. Press Send to: Right Projector button.
  4. Power on the document camera by pressing the ON/OFF button.
  5. Control the settings: Zoom, Contrast and Focus by pressing the buttons on document camera.
  6. Power off the document camera by pressing the ON/OFF button again.
Note: If document camera is not going to be used, please leave it off.
