Videoconference

Videoconference is very popular especially for meetings and job interviews. The software that is commonly used is Adobe Connect, GoToMeeting, Google Hangouts and Skype.

There are built-in cameras already set up in the classrooms, and they are great for this kind of activity. These cameras are connected to the computers set up with the A.V. system.

Adobe Connect, GoToMeeting, Google Hangouts,and Skype are all going to need a camera selected for the video. The option of the built-in camera is going to be available in the software. Once it is selected, people on the other end of the videoconference call are able to see the video.
